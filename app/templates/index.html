Our History

SINCE 1974 Nick Margaritas founded Piano Man in 1974. A pianist with an education in business management, he initially started as a piano service company developing journeymen technicians and hiring personnel interested in honing their skills in piano technology. Nick was the first to import used Yamaha and Kawai pianos from Japan for resale here in America. MusiCrash has grown to be a major buyout specialist of troubled piano companies throughout the country. We have the largest facility on the East Coast and we regularly rebuild, refinish and restore pianos for artists, consumers and all major insurance companies.
